About Our Association

The Nepalese Student Association at Towson University is a vibrant community dedicated to promoting Nepalese culture, fostering friendships, and providing support to Nepalese students and those interested in Nepal. We serve as a home away from home for Nepalese students while introducing the broader TU community to the rich traditions and values of Nepal.

Our Mission

To promote Nepalese culture, foster academic excellence, and build lasting friendships among students from all backgrounds.

Our Vision

To be the leading cultural organization at TU, creating an inclusive environment where diversity is celebrated and understood.

Discover Nepal

Nepal, nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, is a land of ancient traditions, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality. From the majestic Mount Everest to the spiritual temples of Kathmandu, Nepal offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness that has inspired travelers and spiritual seekers for centuries.

The Himalayas

Home to 8 of the world's 10 highest peaks, including Mount Everest (8,848m). The Himalayas offer world-class trekking, mountaineering, and breathtaking views that attract adventurers from around the globe.

Spiritual Heritage

Birthplace of Buddha and center of meditation, Nepal is home to thousands of temples, monasteries, and sacred sites. The country's spiritual traditions continue to influence millions worldwide.

Rich Culture

With over 125 ethnic groups, Nepal boasts diverse traditions in art, music, dance, and festivals. From the colorful Dashain festival to traditional Newari architecture, culture is everywhere.

Natural Diversity

From the tropical Terai plains to the alpine Himalayas, Nepal's diverse ecosystems support unique wildlife including snow leopards, one-horned rhinos, and over 800 species of birds.
